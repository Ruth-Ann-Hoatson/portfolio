Media Studies

Media Studies is a vital module that should not be overlooked in the generation in which we reside. It consists of all mass communication ranging from the intricacies of movie production to social media management. Media studies is recognised as having four main areas of study, including the following:- media language, representation, audience and institution. The media can be used to create meaning through the use of codes such as camera angles, costume, colour and typography. Good media institutions pay careful attention to their audience before creating their media presentation. Media Studies deals with analysing as much as it does with content creation. In this assignment, content will be analysed with particular attention on its meaning, wording and composition. The concept of culture jamming will also be delved into in the last question.

Corporate Communications

Bank On Reading Campaign

The aim of the ‘Bank on Reading Campaign’ is to equip less fortunate children in public schools, within the Western Cape and Gauteng provinces of South Africa, with literacy skills to provide a better future for themselves, their families and South Africans as a whole. Both Help2read and Capitec believe that greater literacy skills set a foundation for a better future.
